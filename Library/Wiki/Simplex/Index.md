# Section: Simplicial Geometry & Grid Architecture

This section documents the discrete coordinate layout, directed causal graphs (substrates), and polynomial state vectors that underpin the simplicial grid architecture.

## Core Concepts

*   **Directed Causal Substrates**: Spacetime coordinates are directed acyclic graphs of causal interactions.
*   **Discrete Chromogeometry**: Coordinates are pixels ($x, y \in \mathbb{Z}$). Distances (Quadrance) and trigonometric ratios (Spread) are exact cross-multiplied integers, eliminating precision loss.
*   **Spread Polynomials**: Governors of spatial angles and resonance filters on the discrete grid.
