# Section: Compound Systems & Chemistry

This section documents how stable composite structures—such as atoms, molecules, and molecular bonds—natively emerge from discrete chromogeometric coordinate locks.

## Core Concepts

In the Primorial Architecture, chemistry is not driven by continuous electromagnetic forces or electronic orbitals. Instead, it is governed by:
*   **Algebraic Valence**: The prime-gate degree of the coordinate state (e.g., Carbon has a valence of 4, matching the Matter-Bond gate).
*   **Geometric Locks**: Pythagorean fixed points (such as the $H_2O$ bond configuration) where spatial quadrance ratios exactly match the gate degrees, resolving fractional charges.
*   **Structural Lag**: Causal delay (mass-inertia) concentrated at coordinate intersections.
