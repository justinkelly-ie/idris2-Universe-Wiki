# Section: Evolution & The Adaptive Cycle

This section documents the clocks, transformations, and scale-transition pipelines that drive the dynamical unfolding of the universe.

## Core Concepts

*   **Adaptive Cycle**: Spacetime does not evolve continuously. It progresses through discrete cycles of **Partitioning** (splitting state pools), **Resonance** (reduction through prime gates), and **Ascension** (gluing stable nodes into a macro-coordinate).
*   **The 210 State Budget**: Enforcing that the overall Primorial state count (Matter, Dark Energy, and Dark Matter background) strictly preserves the 210-state bound ($2 \times 3 \times 5 \times 7$).
